Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is the minimum order quantity for custom branded products?

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the product type and customization method. For most items, we can accommodate orders as small as 25-50 units, while some specialized products may have higher minimums. Contact us with your specific needs, and we'll provide options that work for your budget and requirements.

How long does the branding process typically take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your order and the products selected. Standard orders typically take 2-4 weeks from design approval to delivery. Rush services are available for an additional fee, which can reduce production time to 7-10 days for many products.

What file formats do you require for branding artwork?

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) for the cleanest results, but we can work with high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG, PSD) at 300 DPI or higher. If you don't have suitable artwork, our design team can create custom designs based on your brand guidelines for an additional fee.

Do you offer sustainable or eco-friendly product options?

Yes! We're committed to sustainability and offer a wide range of eco-friendly options including organic cotton apparel, recycled materials, biodegradable products, and items from certified sustainable sources. Let us know your sustainability priorities, and we'll recommend appropriate products.

Can you help with product selection if I'm unsure what would work best?

Absolutely! Our branding consultants are experts at matching products to your specific goals, audience, and budget. We'll ask about your objectives, target demographic, and brand values to recommend the most effective products for your campaign.
